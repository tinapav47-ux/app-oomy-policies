Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2023

1. Information We Collect

OANU camera app processes your photos locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or photos to our servers.

2. Camera and Photos

The app requires camera access to take photos and gallery access to save processed images. All photo processing happens locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

Your photos are stored only on your device. We do not have access to your photos or any personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

OANU does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete the app at any time to remove all associated data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@oanu.app
